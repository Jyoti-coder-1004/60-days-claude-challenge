## 💡 Key Learnings & Takeaways
1. **Context Matters:** Resumes shouldn't be static documents; adapting phrasing to match the vocabulary used in a company's Job Description significantly influences parsing accuracy.
2. **Formatting Simplicity:** Clean, single-column text hierarchies are far superior for ATS compatibility compared to fancy design templates.
3. **Factual Integrity:** Learned how to reframe and restructure existing experience to elevate relevant contributions without fabricating or exaggerating qualifications.
