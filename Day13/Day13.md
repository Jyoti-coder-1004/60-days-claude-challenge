# Day 13: Build Your AI Job Search Assistant 🤖💼

## Project Overview
For today's challenge, I configured **Claude Connectors** to link my Indeed account directly to Claude. Using a sequence of structured prompts, I transformed Claude into a personalized AI job search assistant that automatically discovers matching opportunities, evaluates skill gaps, and analyzes market trends.

---

## 🚀 Job Search Prompts & Strategy

### 1. Professional Profile
I provided Claude with a comprehensive overview of my current background, including:
* **Current Role & Experience:** [e.g., Aspiring Full-Stack Developer / 0-1 years of experience]
* **Core Skills:** [e.g., JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, Git]
* **Location & Achievements:** [e.g., Remote / Ghaziabad, completed several full-stack capstone projects]

### 2. Search Criteria
I instructed Claude to narrow down jobs based on the following preferences:
* **Target Titles:** [e.g., Associate Software Engineer, Frontend Developer, Junior Full-Stack Developer]
* **Work Preference:** Remote or Hybrid
* **Recency:** Posted within the last 14 days

## 💡 Key Learnings
1. **Leveraging AI Connectors:** Learned how to seamlessly connect live external platforms (like Indeed) to LLMs for real-time data analysis.
2. **Data-Driven Job Hunting:** Instead of blindly applying, I learned to prioritize roles using an AI-generated "Match Score."
3. **Targeted Upskilling:** The skill gap analysis gave me a clear, actionable roadmap of what technical skills to learn next to stay competitive.
