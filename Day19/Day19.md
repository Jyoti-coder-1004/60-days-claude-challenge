# Day 19: Build a Football Intelligence Hub

## 📋 Project Overview
Today's challenge focused on leveraging Claude's analytical, educational, and profiling abilities to build a multi-stage **Football Intelligence Hub**. Using a specialized football workbook dataset, Claude acted as an analyst, educator, and personality assessor.

---

## 🛠️ Task Execution Steps
1. **Setup & Configuration**: Opened Claude and toggled the effort level to **Low**.
2. **Data Integration**: Uploaded the downloaded football workbook from the provided resources.
3. **Execution**: Pasted the comprehensive Football Intelligence Hub prompt to kick off the multi-stage interaction.
4. **Interactive Journey**:
   - Completed the **Knowledge Level Check** to calibrate response depth.
   - Reviewed the data-driven **FIFA World Cup 2026 Prediction Report**.
   - Attempted the adaptive **Football IQ Quiz**.
   - Completed the **Messi vs Ronaldo Personality Match** assessment.
5. **Documentation**: Captured screenshots of the final **Football Intelligence Profile** and saved the outputs.
