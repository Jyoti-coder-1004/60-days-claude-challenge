# Day 21: Digital Privacy Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
An interactive, premium cybersecurity-themed dashboard built to visualize digital footprints, track cross-platform data exposure risks, and generate actionable privacy improvement plans. It maps out 15 everyday applications to their respective parent companies to evaluate tracking surfaces and ecosystem concentrations.

---

## 🛠️ Key Learnings
* **Ecosystem Concentration Mapping:** Learned how a handful of parent companies (Meta, Alphabet) consolidate and process behavioral telemetry from seemingly disconnected apps.
* **Fact vs. Estimate Separation:** Practiced strict data segregation—ensuring known applications are treated as factual constraints while behavioral/lifestyle traits are clearly signaled as statistical inferences.
* **Interactive UI Simulation:** Built dynamic scoring systems in JavaScript that immediately recalculate privacy scores based on user mitigation simulation rules.

---

## 📊 Privacy Findings & Analysis
* **Digital Footprint Score:** Calculated as **Extensive (81-100)** due to the wide combination of entertainment, messaging, search, and fintech applications.
* **Privacy Score:** Evaluated as **Weak to Fair (0-60)** without active mitigations, heavily weighted by cross-app cross-referencing capabilities from modern data brokers.
* **Top Exposure Risks:** Concentrated tightly around Alphabet (Google Services) and Meta (Instagram, WhatsApp) platforms owing to persistent geolocation tracking, cross-site trackers, and transaction visibility.
