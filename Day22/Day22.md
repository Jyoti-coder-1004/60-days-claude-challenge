# Day 22: Validate Your Startup Idea Like a VC

## 📋 Task Overview
Today's challenge focused on turning raw startup assumptions into evidence-based, VC-grade insights using advanced AI prompting. By acting as a Startup Advisor and VC Analyst, I thoroughly evaluated a startup concept, calculated market sizes (TAM, SAM, SOM), mapped out buyer personas, and formulated a rigorous 30-day execution roadmap.

---

## 🧠 Key Learnings
* **Problem Validation over Product Building:** Validating whether a problem is painful enough to solve saves months of wasted engineering effort. 
* **Granular Market Sizing:** Breaking down the Total Addressable Market (TAM) into Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM) and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) provides a realistic view of the initial beachhead market.
* **Founder-Market Fit:** Understanding my personal alignment, background, and unfair advantages regarding the problem space helps de-risk the execution phase.
* **Objective Guardrails:** Establishing clear Go / No-Go criteria eliminates emotional bias when deciding whether to pivot or persevere.
