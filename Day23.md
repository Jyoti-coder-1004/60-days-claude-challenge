## 💡 Key Learnings
1. **Scope Creep is Dangerous:** Realized that over 50% of the features initially planned were distractions that didn't serve the immediate core pain point.
2. **Focus on Triggers:** Understanding the exact event that forces a user to search for a tool is more valuable than just knowing their demographics.
3. **Data over Assumptions:** Building only what is required to validate the *Must-Haves* saves weeks of wasted engineering hours.
