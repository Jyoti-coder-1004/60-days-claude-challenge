## 📌 Overview
Today, I explored **Context Engineering**, a technique centered around providing specific background information, constraints, and guardrails to AI models. Instead of relying on generic prompts, supplying explicit context (such as roles, skills, goals, and availability) transforms cookie-cutter AI responses into highly tailored, practical, and execution-ready blueprints.
